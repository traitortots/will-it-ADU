About

A New Picture of Segregation is part of an ongoing research project headed by Wenfei Xu.

The aim of this research is to develop a better understanding of how new types of spatiotemporal data may help us gain a better understanding of how human mobility data can improve our understanding of segregation and neighborhood change.

This website was created by Allie Chu and Wenfei Xu.


To get in touch, please email wenfei@uchicago.edu
